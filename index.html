We are re-architecting the environment of incentives for healthcare claims

With BitClaims Payers and Providers engage in a streamlined health insurance claims solution using blockchain and smart contract technology.

The BitClaims Mission

Rather than expend a tremendous amount of resources policing healthcare claims, BitClaims creates an environment where claims police themselves.

Decentralized

Payers and Providers enter into an agreement knowing all the terms of execution, of which no one party can change at will. BitClaims nodes run on the machines of network participants for Payers, Providers, Billers and Coders. All BitClaims network participants verify the specific metrics and variables that are entered into the fields of a claim in real time.

Transparent

All network participants come to a consensus on the work being done on each claim in real time. Pre-populated smart contracts mapped to claim specific procedural and diagnostic codes ensure an audited, verified and adjudicated claim. Stay tuned for our demo to show how we are automating out clearinghouses!

Compliant

Each process in the claims life cycle meets all CMS, LCD, NCD and EOB requirements. The BitClaims platform enables a top down approach that ensures each claim is meeting federal, state and local requirements for a claim. With the network coming to a shared consensus, each claim is then linked to execute with smart contracts matching the payers EOB.

R.A.N.S.

Receipt Aggregate of Net Sale

A unique take on crowd sourcing working capital for the BitClaims core business functions that enables donators to become members of a participant group which holds receipts that are tied to the direct cash flow of the BitClaims platform.
